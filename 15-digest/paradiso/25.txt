# Canto 25 — The Vision of the Three Lights and the Revelation of Hope

## Scene 1-12: Dante's Prologue
en: While in the *ramo*, *Dante* hopes his sacred poem will overcome the cruelty of his exile and lead him home. He recalls his entry into the faith of *Dio* and the blessing *Pietro* bestowed upon him because of *Beatrice*.
ja: 枝に身を置きながら、Danteは自らの聖なる詩が追放の残酷さを乗り越え、自分を故郷へと導いてくれることを願った。彼は、Dioへの信仰に就いたこと、そしてBeatriceゆえにPietroから授かった祝福を回想する。

## Scene 13-18: The Appearance of the Second Light
en: In the *ramo*, *Giacomo* appears as a light from the sphere where *San Pietro* had left *Cristo* and *i vicari suoi*. *Beatrice*, filled with joy, tells *Dante* to look upon him.
ja: 枝の中に、San PietroがCristoとi vicari suoiを置いてきた天球から、Giacomoが光となって現れる。歓喜に満ちたBeatriceは、Danteに彼を見るよう促した。

## Scene 19-24: Simile of the Two Princes
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* observes the affection between *Giacomo* and *Pietro* and their praise for the food provided by *Beatrice* to *i beati*.
ja: 枝にて、DanteはGiacomoとPietroが分かち合う情愛と、Beatriceがi beatiに授けた食物への彼らの賛辞を静かに見守った。

## Scene 25-33: Beatrice's Invocation of Hope
en: After *'l gratular* concludes in the *ramo*, *San Pietro* and *San Giacomo* gaze upon *Dante* with blinding light, and *Bëatrice* asks *San Giacomo* to sound his spear, reminding him that *Iesù* especially loved him, *San Pietro*, and *San Giovanni*.
ja: 'l gratular がその枝において締めくくられると、San Pietro と San Giacomo が眩い光とともに Dante を見つめ、Bëatrice は Iesù が彼と San Pietro、そして San Giovanni を格別に愛していたことを思い出させながら、San Giacomo に槍を鳴らすよう求めた。

## Scene 34-36: Beatrice's Command
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* is told to lift his head and be reassured that those ascending from below must be matured by the rays of light.
ja: 枝分かれした道にて、Danteは顔を上げ、下から登り来る者たちは光の光線によって熟成されねばならぬのだと諭され、心を安んじた。

## Scene 37-47: The Second Light's Inquiry
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* is comforted and looks up as he is told that by grace he may face *lo nostro Imperadore* and *suoi conti*. He is then asked to explain the nature of the spear and how it came to his mind.
ja: その枝道にてDanteは心を慰められ、恩寵によってlo nostro Imperadoreとsuoi contiに謁見できると告げられて顔を上げた。そして、その槍の正体と、それがどのようにして心に浮かんだのかを説明するよう求められた。

## Scene 48-63: The Nature of Hope in the Church
en: In the *ramo*, *Beatrice* answers *Giacomo* on *Dante*'s behalf, declaring that *Dante* possesses more hope than any other son of *La Chiesa militante* as written in *Dio*. She then leaves the remaining points of the question for *Dante* to answer himself, praying that the grace of *Dio* enable him to do so.
ja: 枝の上で、BeatriceはDanteに代わってGiacomoに答え、Dioに記されている通り、DanteがLa Chiesa militanteのいかなる子よりも大きな希望を抱いていることを宣言した。そして、問いの残りの部分はDante自身に委ね、Dioの恩寵が彼にそれを叶えんことを祈った。

## Scene 64-78: Dante's Scholarly Response
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* answers *Giacomo il Maggiore* like an eager student, defining hope as the certain expectation of future glory. He explains that this grace was first distilled into him by *Virgilio*, the singer of *Augusto*, who spoke of *Dio* and of both *color che sanno il nome tuo* and *chi nol sa*, and was further completed by the writings of *Giacomo il Maggiore*.
ja: 枝の上で、Danteは熱心な生徒のようにGiacomo il Maggioreに答え、希望とは未来の栄光への確かな期待であると定義した。この恩寵は、Dio、そしてcolor che sanno il nome tuoとchi nol saについて語ったAugustoの歌い手Virgilioによってまず彼に注がれ、さらにGiacomo il Maggioreの著作によって完成されたのだと彼は説明した。

## Scene 79-87: The Third Light's Command
en: In the *ramo*, *Giovanni* suddenly appears as a flash of light and asks *Dante* to describe what hope promises him, sharing the love he feels for the virtue that accompanied him to his reward.
ja: 枝の中にGiovanniが不意に光の閃光となって現れ、希望が自分に何を約束しているのかを語るようDanteに請い、自らを報いへと導いた美徳への愛を分かち合った。

## Scene 88-96: Scriptural Identification of Hope
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* explains that the writings of *Isaia* and *San Pietro* confirm the hope of the *anime* made friends of *Dio*, a revelation manifest to *Dante, i fedeli*.
ja: その枝において、Danteは、IsaiaとSan Pietroの記述がDioの友となったanimeたちの希望を裏付けていると説く。それはDante、すなわちi fedeliに示された啓示であった。

## Scene 97-99: The Heavenly Response
en: In the *ramo*, just as *Dante* finishes his words, a voice from *Dio* sounds from above *Dante*, *Beatrice*, *San Pietro*, *San Giacomo*, and *San Giovanni*, to which *tutte le carole* respond.
ja: 枝の上でDanteが言葉を終えたそのとき、Dioの声がDante、Beatrice、San Pietro、San Giacomo、そしてSan Giovanniの頭上に響き渡り、それにtutte le caroleが唱和した。

## Scene 100-108: The Arrival of the Third Light
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* watches a brilliant light approach *San Pietro* and *San Giacomo*, who turn to meet it in love. This light is likened to a *vergine lieta* who joins a dance to honor a novice.
ja: 枝の中で、Danteはまばゆい光がSan PietroとSan Giacomoへと近づき、二人が愛に満ちてそれに向き直るさまを眺めていた。その光は、新入りの者を祝う舞いへと加わるvergine lietaに例えられていた。

## Scene 109-114: Beatrice's Identification
en: In the *ramo*, *Giovanni* joins *Pietro* and *Giacomo* in the dance while *Beatrice* watches and identifies *Giovanni* as the one who rested on the breast of *Cristo* and was chosen for the great office at the cross.
ja: 枝の中でGiovanniがPietroとGiacomoの舞いへと加わるなか、Beatriceはその姿を見つめ、GiovanniこそがCristoの胸に寄り添い、十字架における大いなる職務に選ばれた者であると説いた。

## Scene 115-117: Beatrice's Silent Attention
en: In the *ramo*, *Beatrice* remains silent and attentive, her gaze unwavering after she has spoken.
ja: 枝の上で、Beatriceは静かに、注意深く佇んでいた。語り終えた後も、その眼差しが揺らぐことはなかった。

## Scene 118-129: The Prophecy of the Two Lights
en: In the *ramo*, *Dante* compares his blindness to that of *colui ch'adocchia*, and *San Giovanni* tells him that while his own body is earth and he dwells with *li altri*, *Dante* shall carry the revelation of him, *San Pietro*, and *San Giacomo* back to the world.
ja: 枝の中で、Danteは自らの盲目をcolui ch'adocchiaのそれに重ね合わせた。それに対しSan Giovanniは、自らの肉体は土に還りli altriと共に在るが、Danteは彼自身とSan Pietro、そしてSan Giacomoの啓示を、再び世界へと運ぶことになるのだと告げた。

## Scene 130-135: The Settling of the Heavens
en: In the *ramo*, a voice brings a sudden silence to the surrounding music and light, which *Dante* compares to oars stopping at the sound of a whistle.
ja: 枝の中で、ある声が周囲の音楽と光を突如として静まり返らせた。Dante はその様子を、笛の合図に櫂が止まるさまに例えている。

## Scene 136-139: Dante's Final Vision
en: In the *mondo felice*, *Dante* is deeply shaken when he turns to look for *Beatrice* but finds he cannot see her, despite being right beside her.
ja: 至福の世界において、DanteはBeatriceを探して振り返るが、すぐ隣にいるはずの彼女の姿が見えないことに、激しく心を揺さぶられた。
