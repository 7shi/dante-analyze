# Canto 12 — The Praise of Saint Dominic and the Gathering of the Saints

## Scene 1-9: The Rotation of the Celestial Gear
en: As soon as *Tommaso* is finished speaking, *ella* begins to rotate in harmony with the motion above, producing a song that far surpasses the music of the *muse*.
ja: Tommasoが話し終えた途端、彼女は上方の動きと調和して回転し始め、museの音楽を遥かに凌駕する歌声を奏でた。

## Scene 10-21: The Intertwining Garlands
en: Two garlands of roses rotate around *Dante* and *Beatrice* in *ella*, the outer responding to the inner like the rainbow associated with *Iunone* and *Iride*, or the covenant *Dio* made with *Noè* to reassure the *gente*.
ja: 二本の薔薇の花冠がその中でDanteとBeatriceの周囲を巡り、外側の輪が内側の輪に呼応して回っている。それは、IunoneとIrideに結びついた虹か、あるいはDioがgenteを安んじるためにNoèと結んだ契約のごとき光景であった。

## Scene 22-30: The Voice Emerges
en: As the joyful singing and flashing of light among *le luci nove* in *ella* simultaneously cease, a voice emerges from one of them, pulling *Dante* toward it like a compass needle to a star.
ja: le luci nove の間で奏でられていた歓喜の歌と光のまたたきが同時に止むと、そのうちの一つから声が沸き起こり、Dante はあたかも磁針が星に惹きつけられるかのように、その声へと引き寄せられた。

## Scene 31-36: The Theology of the Two Guides
en: Within *ella*, a voice speaks of *San Domenico* and *Virgilio*, asserting that since *Beatrice* is present, *Virgilio* should also be led there so their glory may shine together as praised by *i beati*.
ja: 彼女の内で、ある声が San Domenico と Virgilio について語り、Beatrice がそこにいる以上、Virgilio もまたそこへ導かれ、i beati が称える通りに彼らの栄光が共に輝くべきだと説いていた。

## Scene 37-45: The Allegory of the Church's Defense
en: Within `*ella*`, `*Cristo*` is spoken of as granting grace to those who faltered and sending `*Domenico*` and `*Francesco*` as champions to lead them back.
ja: `ella`の中では、`Cristo`が躓いた者たちに恩寵を授け、彼らを導き戻すための救い手として`Domenico`と`Francesco`を遣わしたと語られている。

## Scene 46-60: The Birth of Saint Dominic
en: Within `*ella*`, a voice speaks of the birth of `*San Domenico*` in `*Europa*`, where the sun sometimes hides from `*ogne uom*`, describing him as a holy champion who was stern toward `*nemici*`. It is mentioned that the virtue with which `*San Domenico*` was created made `*la madre*` a prophet.
ja: その中で、ある声が、太陽が時に`ogne uom`から姿を隠す`Europa`における`San Domenico`の誕生を語り、彼を`nemici`に厳格な聖なる勇者として描き出している。また、`San Domenico`が備えていた徳が`la madre`を預言者たらしめたという。

## Scene 61-78: The Vision and Signs of Sanctity
en: Within *ella*, *Domenico* recounts how *la donna* dreamed of the child resulting from her and *il padre di Domenico*, and how *spirito* gave him his name. He describes his calling to serve *Cristo* and how *la sua nutrice* often found him as a child in divine trances.
ja: ellaの内で、Domenicoは、la donnaが彼女とil padre di Domenicoとの間に授かった子の夢を見たこと、そしてspiritoが彼に名を授けたことを語る。また、Cristoに仕えるという自らの召命や、幼い頃にla sua nutriceがしばしば彼を神聖な恍惚状態で見つけたことについて述べている。

## Scene 79-87: The Rapid Growth of the Doctor
en: Within `*ella*`, `*Domenico*` is spoken of as praising `*Felice*` and `*Giovanna*` and as having rapidly become a great doctor, unlike those who followed `*Agostino*` and `*Taddeo*`. He is described as a diligent worker who prevents the vineyard from whitening through negligence.
ja: `ella` の中で、`Domenico` は `Felice` と `Giovanna` を称賛し、`Agostino` や `Taddeo` に従った者たちとは異なり、急速に名医となった人物として語られている。彼は、怠慢によって葡萄園が白く枯れるのを防ぐ、勤勉な働き手であると記されている。

## Scene 88-105: The Missionary Zeal of Dominic
en: Within `*ella*`, `*San Domenico*` tells how he sought from `*il Papa*` not the privileges of `*i Francescani*` or the tithes of `*pauperum Dei*`, but the license to fight for the `*Parola di Dio*`. He then surged against `*li sterpi eretici*` like a torrent, establishing the `*Ordine domenicano*` to nourish `*i suoi arbuscelli*`.
ja: `ella`において、`San Domenico`は、`il Papa`に求めたのは`i Francescani`の特権でも`pauperum Dei`の什一税でもなく、`Parola di Dio`のために戦う許しであったと語る。彼はその後、奔流となって`li sterpi eretici`に立ち向かい、`i suoi arbuscelli`を育むために`Ordine domenicano`を創設した。

## Scene 106-114: The Vacancy in the Second Circle
en: Within `*ella*`, a voice tells `*Dante*` that the excellence of another is evident, as `*Tomma*` had spoken of, but observes that a part of the orbit is now vacant.
ja: そのなかで、ある声が Dante に告げた。Tomma が語った通り、ある者の卓越性は明白であるが、いまや軌道の一部が空席となっている、と。

## Scene 115-126: The Vastness of the Second Ring
en: Within `*ella*`, *San Domenico* describes how *i Francescani* rotate such that *un'anima tra i Francescani* may shift from leading to following, and warns that *il loglio* will lament being excluded from the ark. He asserts that his identity is documented in his order's records, unlike the divergent paths of *i frati di Casal e Acquasparta*.
ja: その内で、San Domenicoはi Francescaniが交代し、un'anima tra i Francescaniが導く側から導かれる側へと移り変わるさまを説き、il loglioが方舟から除かれたことを嘆くことになると警告する。そして、道を違えたi frati di Casal e Acquaspartaとは異なり、自らの正体は会派の記録に刻まれているのだと断言した。

## Scene 127-141: The Assembly of the Saints
en: *Domenico* is spoken of as the life of *Bonaventura* and as a friend to the *primi scalzi poverelli* who served *Dio*. Within *ella*, *Bonaventura*, *Illuminato*, *Augustin*, *Ugo da San Vittore*, *Pietro Mangiadore*, *Pietro Spano*, *Natàn*, *Crisostomo*, *Anselmo*, *Donato*, *Rabano*, and *Giovacchino* are all gathered.
ja: DomenicoはBonaventuraの命であり、Dioに仕えしprimi scalzi poverelliの友であると語られている。その中には、Bonaventura、Illuminato、Augustin、Ugo da San Vittore、Pietro Mangiadore、Pietro Spano、Natàn、Crisostomo、Anselmo、Donato、Rabano、そしてGiovacchinoが皆集っていた。

## Scene 142-145: The Unified Praise
en: Within *ella*, *Domenico* explains that the courtesy of *Tommaso* and *Virgilio* prompted him to praise *Francesco* and brought *Dante* and the mentioned *Beatrice* to him.
ja: ellaの中で、Domenicoは、TommasoとVirgilioの礼節に促されてFrancescoを称賛し、それがDanteと前述のBeatriceを自身のもとへと導いたのだと説明した。
