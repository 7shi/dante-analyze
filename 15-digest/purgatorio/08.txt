# Canto 08 — The Arrival of the Angels and the Encounter with the Malaspina

## Scene 1-18: The Evening Hymn
en: At the hour that brings longing to `*navicanti*` for their `*dolci amici*`, `*una de l'alme*` at the `*fianco de la lacca*` begins a hymn to `*Dio*` that leaves `*Dante*` entranced. `*l'altre*` soon join her in the song, their eyes fixed on the heavens.
ja: `navicanti` が `dolci amici` を恋しく想う時刻になると、湖のほとりにいた `una de l'alme` が `Dio` への賛歌を歌い始め、`Dante` はうっとりと聞き惚れた。間もなく `l'altre` もその歌声に唱和し、その眼差しは一様に天へと向けられた。

## Scene 19-30: The Descent of the Angels
en: *Dante* urges the `*lettor*` to look closely as he watches `*quell'essercito gentile*` wait in humble silence at the `*fianco de la lacca*`. Then, `*due angeli*` in green robes descend, carrying blunt swords.
ja: Danteは、湖のほとりで謙虚な沈黙に身を任せて待つquell'essercito gentileを眺め、lettorに注視するように促した。やがて、緑の衣を纏い、鈍い剣を携えたdue angeliが舞い降りた。

## Scene 31-36: The Crowd's Reaction
en: *l'un* hovers above *Dante*, *Virgilio*, and *Sordello* while *L'altro* descends to the opposite bank, leaving the *gente* in the middle at the *fianco de la lacca*. *Dante* discerns the blond hair of the *angeli*, but the intensity of their faces overwhelms his sight.
ja: l'unがDante、Virgilio、Sordelloの上空に漂う一方で、L'altroは中間にいるgenteを湖の岸辺に残して対岸へと降りていった。Danteはangeliの金髪を認めたものの、その表情の強烈さに視界を圧倒された。

## Scene 37-45: Sordello's Warning
en: At the `*fianco de la lacca*`, `*Sordello*` explains that the `*angeli*` come from `*Maria*` to stand guard, causing a terrified `*Dante*` to cling to `*Virgilio*`. `*Sordello*` then suggests they move to converse with `*le grandi ombre*`.
ja: fianco de la laccaにおいて、Sordelloがangeliは守護のためにMariaから遣わされたのだと説明すると、恐怖に震えるDanteはVirgilioにしがみついた。その後、Sordelloはle grandi ombreと語らうために移動することを提案した。

## Scene 46-55: Meeting the Gentle Judge
en: Descending a few steps at the *fianco de la lacca*, *Dante* approaches *Nin*, who had been watching him intently. *Dante* tells *Nin* how glad he is that he is not among the *rei*, and the two exchange heartfelt greetings.
ja: 湖のほとりの階段を数段降り、Danteは自分をじっと見つめていたNinに歩み寄った。Danteは、Ninがreiの中にいないことを何より嬉しく思うと伝え、二人は心からの挨拶を交わした。

## Scene 56-63: The Inquiry of Origins
en: *Nin* asks *Dante* how long he has been at the *fianco de la lacca*, and upon learning that *Dante* is still in his first life, *Sordello* and *le grandi ombre* recoil in sudden bewilderment.
ja: NinがDanteにfianco de la laccaにどれほどの期間いるのかを尋ねたところ、Danteがまだ最初の生を生きていることが分かり、Sordelloとle grandi ombreは不意に当惑してたじろいだ。

## Scene 64-81: The Message for Giovanna
en: At the *fianco de la lacca*, *Sordello* turns to *Virgilio* and *Nin* calls *Currado da Palazzo* to witness the grace of *Dio*. *Nin* asks *Dante* to tell *Giovanna* to pray for him among *li 'nnocenti*, while reflecting on the fading love of *la sua madre*, the nature of *Femmina*, and the burial provided by *il gallo di Gallura*.
ja: 漆の傍らで、SordelloはVirgilioへと向き、NinはDioの慈悲を証するようにCurrado da Palazzoを呼んだ。Ninは、la sua madreの薄れゆく愛やFemminaの本質、そしてil gallo di Galluraによる埋葬に思いを馳せながら、li 'nnocentiの中で自分のために祈るようGiovannaに伝えてほしいとDanteに請うた。

## Scene 82-93: Celestial Observations
en: At the `*fianco de la lacca*`, `*Dante*` gazes intently at the heavens, and `*Virgilio*` explains to him the shifting positions of the stars.
ja: `fianco de la lacca`にて、`Dante`が天を凝視するなか、`Virgilio`は彼に星々の移ろいゆく位置について説いた。

## Scene 94-108: The Serpent's Intrusion
en: While *Virgilio* speaks, *Sordello* draws *Dante* to look at *l nostro avversaro* in *la picciola vallea*, a creature *Dante* compares to the one who tempted *Eva*. At the sound of the wings of *li angeli*, *l nostro avversaro* flees, and *li angeli* fly back to their stations.
ja: Virgilioが語る間、SordelloはDanteを導き、小さな谷にいるl nostro avversaroを見せた。それはDanteがEvaを誘惑した者に例えた生き物であったが、li angeliの羽音が響くとl nostro avversaroは逃げ去り、li angeliは自らの持ち場へと戻っていった。

## Scene 109-120: The Identification of Currado
en: At *la picciola vallea*, *Dante* gazes at *Currado Malaspina*, who had gathered around the mentioned *Nin*. *Currado Malaspina* asks *Dante* for news of his home and acknowledges the guidance *Virgilio* provides.
ja: la picciola valleaにて、Danteは前述のNinを囲んでいたCurrado Malaspinaに目を留めた。Currado MalaspinaはDanteに故郷の知らせを求め、Virgilioの導きに謝意を示した。

## Scene 121-132: Praise for the Malaspina
en: At *la picciola vallea*, *Dante* tells *Currado Malaspina* that although he has never visited his lands, the honor of his house is renowned among *i segnori* throughout Europe. He swears that while *il capo reo* twists the world, the lineage of *Currado Malaspina* remains upright in both generosity and courage.
ja: その小さな谷で、DanteはCurrado Malaspinaに、その地を訪れたことはないが、彼の家の誉れは欧州中のi segnoriの間で名高いことを告げた。そして、il capo reoが世界を歪めている間も、Currado Malaspinaの血統は寛大さと勇気を持ち、正道を歩み続けていると誓った。

## Scene 133-139: The Judge's Dismissal
en: At *la picciola vallea*, *Dante* is urged to move on before the sun circles seven times in the bed of the ram, lest his praise for the house of *Nin* be nailed to his head.
ja: 小さな谷にて、Danteは、Ninの家への賛辞が自らの頭に釘付けにされることのないよう、太陽が牡羊の座を七度巡る前に先へ進むよう促される。
