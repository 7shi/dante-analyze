# Canto 27 — The Confession of the Second Flame

## Scene 1-15: The Sound of the Flame
en: At *'l ponte*, the flame of *Ulisse* ceases speaking and departs from *Dante* and *Virgilio*, but a new flame approaches, emitting a distorted sound reminiscent of the cries of *l'afflitto*.
ja: 橋において、Ulisse の炎は語るのをやめて Dante と Virgilio から離れていったが、代わって新たな炎が近づき、l'afflitto の叫びを彷彿とさせる歪んだ音を響かせた。

## Scene 16-30: The Inquiry of the Soul
en: At *'l ponte*, *l'anima*'s voice finally becomes clear, and the soul asks *Dante* to stay and provide news of whether there is peace or war in their homeland.
ja: 'l ponteにて、l'animaの声がついに明瞭となり、その魂はDanteに留まることを求め、故郷に平和があるのか、あるいは戦火にあるのかを知らせてほしいと願った。

## Scene 31-48: Dante's Political Report
en: At *'l ponte*, *Virgilio* prompts *Dante* to answer *l'anima*, who learns that their homeland is ravaged by *tiranni*. *Dante* speaks of the blood of the *Franceschi* and the predatory rule of *Malatesta Vecchio* and *Malatesta Giovane*.
ja: 橋にて、Virgilio に促された Dante が l'anima に答えると、l'anima は故郷が tiranni によって蹂躙されていることを知る。Dante は Franceschi の流した血と、Malatesta Vecchio および Malatesta Giovane による略奪的な統治について語った。

## Scene 49-57: The Geography of the Region
en: At `*'l ponte*`, `*Dante*` describes the political instability surrounding `*il lïoncel*` and `*il Savio*`, then begs `*Guido da Montefeltro*` to reveal his identity.
ja: `'l ponte` において、`Dante` は `il lïoncel` と `il Savio` をめぐる政治的な混迷について述べ、次いで `Guido da Montefeltro` にその正体を明かすよう請うた。

## Scene 58-66: The Flame's Vow of Truth
en: After the flame's tip flickers, the soul tells *Dante* that because it believes no one ever returns alive from the *fondo*, it can reveal the truth without fear of infamy.
ja: 炎の先端が揺らめくと、その魂は Dante に、底から生きて戻れる者はいないと信じているため、悪名を恐れることなく真実を明かせると告げた。

## Scene 67-78: The Nature of the Deceiver
en: At `*fondo*` in `*'l ponte*`, `*l'anima della seconda fiamma*` explains to `*Dante*` that he had attempted to atone for his early life, only to be led back to sin by `*il gran prete*`. He confesses that from the moment he was born of `*la madre*`, he lived with the guile of a fox rather than the bravery of a lion, becoming a master of deception.
ja: 橋の底において、`l'anima della seconda fiamma` は `Dante` に対し、かつての人生を贖おうと試みたものの、`il gran prete` によって再び罪へと誘い戻されたことを明かす。彼は、`la madre` より生を受けたその時から、獅子の勇猛さではなく狐の狡猾さをもって生き、欺瞞の達人となったことを告白した。

## Scene 79-93: The History of Deceit
en: At *fondo* in *'l ponte*, *Guido da Montefeltro* explains that he had repented in his later years, only to be lured back into sin by *Bonifacio VIII*, who disregarded his own sacred office and the vows of the penitent to serve his own political wars.
ja: 橋の底でGuido da Montefeltroは、晩年にこそ悔い改めたものの、自らの聖職と悔悟者の誓いさえも顧みず私的な政治闘争に利用しようとしたBonifacio VIIIによって、再び罪へと誘い込まれたことを語る。

## Scene 94-105: The Pope's Counselor
en: At *fondo*, *the soul in the second flame* recounts how *Bonifacio VIII* sought his counsel to satisfy a proud ambition, comparing the request to how *Costantin* once sought *Silvestro* at *Siratti*. To secure his help in overthrowing *Penestrino*, *Bonifacio VIII* offered him absolution and boasted of a divine authority that *Celestino V* had failed to prize.
ja: 底にて、the soul in the second flameは、Bonifacio VIIIが傲慢な野心を満たすために助言を求めたことを、かつてCostantinがSirattiでSilvestroを訪ねたことに例えて語った。Penestrinoを打倒するための協力を得るべく、Bonifacio VIIIは彼に赦免を提示し、Celestino Vが顧みなかった神聖なる権威を誇示したのである。

## Scene 106-111: The Dangerous Advice
en: At *fondo*, *Guido da Montefeltro* decides that silence is the worst course of action and advises *Bonifacio VIII* that a deceptive strategy of long promises and short waiting will ensure his triumph in the high office.
ja: 深淵にて、Guido da Montefeltroは沈黙こそが最悪の策であると悟り、Bonifacio VIIIに、長い約束と短い待機という欺瞞に満ちた策略こそが高位の座での勝利を確実にするだろうと説いた。

## Scene 112-123: The Cherub's Intervention
en: At *fondo*, *Francesco* attempts to intercede for *l'anima*, but *un d'i neri cherubini* blocks him, asserting that *l'anima* belongs among *i miei meschini* because his lack of true repentance forbids absolution. *l'anima* recoils as *un d'i neri cherubini* seizes it, mockingly claiming to be a master of logic.
ja: 最深部にて、Francescoはl'animaのためにとりなそうとするが、un d'i neri cherubiniがそれを阻み、真の悔悟を欠くl'animaに赦しはなく、i miei meschiniに属すべきであると断じた。un d'i neri cherubiniが自らを論理の達人と嘲笑いながらl'animaを掴むと、l'animaは身をすくませた。

## Scene 124-129: The Judgment of Minos
en: At *fondo*, *Minòs* judges *l'anima della seconda fiamma*, wrapping his tail eight times and declaring the soul to be one of *i rei del foco*. *L'anima della seconda fiamma* then explains to *Dante* that this is why it is now lost and wrapped in flame.
ja: 底にて、Minòsは尾を八回巻きつけ、l'anima della seconda fiammaをi rei del focoの一人であると裁いた。するとl'anima della seconda fiammaはDanteに、それゆえに自分は今、迷い、炎に包まれているのだと説明した。

## Scene 130-136: The Departure of the Flame
en: Once the flame concludes its confession and departs in agony, *Dante* and *Virgilio* proceed across the rocky ledge of the *fondo* toward the next arch.
ja: 炎が告白を終え、苦悶のうちに去っていくと、DanteとVirgilioは底部の岩棚を渡り、次のアーチへと歩みを進めた。
